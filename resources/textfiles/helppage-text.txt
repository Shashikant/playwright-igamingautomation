About the game
You've been given a chance to see a unique magic slot machine that can multiply your bet! Apart from the
traditional slot playing there is a Bonus game. where the aim is to collect bonus symbols. Depending on the
number of these symbols you will receive a multiplier applied to your bet. And on the whole. the slot will favor
you with festive and alluring vibes of a bright holiday.
How to play
The game features win lines. Choose the bet size using the buttons in the Total bet field Higher bets will
increase a total win. The bet value chosen is displayed in the corresponding field. To start the reels spinning.
click the Spin button or the lever of the machine
